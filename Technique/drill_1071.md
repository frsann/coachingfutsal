# Drill 1071

**Short description:**
FUTSAL - TECHNICAL FUNDAMENTALS WITH THE BALL - Authors - Roberto Osimani and Mico Martic

**Objectives:**
Ball control: ball on the ground - in motion.

**Area (mxm):**


**Duration (min):**
From 5' to 15'.

**Number of players:**
At least 6 players: divided into 2 groups.

**Materials:**
1 ball for each player. Cones: to form two rows of cones.

**Progression:**
The two groups start the exercise simultaneously. By performing a slalom, they lead the ball diagonally. At the end of the course, the two groups switch sides. CN09.1 - Outside - inside. CN09.2 - Outside - instep. CN09.3 - Outside - inside - outside. CN09.4 - Outside - instep - outside. POINTS OF ATTENTION: Focus on the execution of the technical gesture.

**Variations:**


**Categories:**
Technique - Control and ball protection
