# Drill 1149

**Short description:**
FUTSAL - TECHNICAL FUNDAMENTALS WITH BALL - Authors - Roberto Osimani and Mico Martic

**Objectives:**
Shot: a 2-player game. Parallel.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
From 5' to 20'

**Number of players:**
At least 8 players: 6 outfield players, 2 goalkeepers.

**Materials:**
Various balls.

**Progression:**
The game is played on half of the field, with all players positioned at the corners of the field. The players are divided into two groups, which take turns performing the exercise. A player passes the ball horizontally to a teammate and then runs parallel to the pass. The teammate, upon receiving the ball, passes it along the line to enable a shot on goal. Immediately after, a player from the other group begins the same movement, and so on. TR09.1 ? Parallel: direct shot. TR09.2 ? Parallel: indirect shot. POINTS OF FOCUS: The pass should be made to the teammate in motion.

**Variations:**
In the indirect parallel, the player passes the ball backward with the sole to let the approaching teammate kick it.

**Categories:**
Technique - Shooting
