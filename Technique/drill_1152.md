# Drill 1152

**Short description:**
FUTSAL - BASIC TECHNIQUES WITH BALL - Authors - Roberto Osimani and Mico Martic

**Objectives:**
Shot: depending on the type of pass. Shot on closure at the 2nd post.

**Area (mxm):**


**Duration (min):**
From 5' to 20'

**Number of players:**
At least 3 players: 2 outfield players, 1 goalkeeper.

**Materials:**
Various balls. Cones: create a line about 10 meters from the goal.

**Progression:**
The goalkeeper has the ball in hand. The two forwards head towards the goal; when they are at the 10-meter line, the goalkeeper passes the ball (as he pleases) to one of the forwards. The player who receives the ball must pass it or shoot at the goal on the second touch, while the other player without the ball must move to cover the second post. POINTS OF ATTENTION: The player with the ball, on the second touch, can either shoot at the goal or pass the ball to his teammate at the second post.

**Variations:**
The exercise can be performed on both halves of the field.

**Categories:**
Technique - Shoeing
