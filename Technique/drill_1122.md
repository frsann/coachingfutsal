# Drill 1122

**Short description:**
FUTSAL - TECHNICAL FUNDAMENTALS WITH THE BALL - Authors - Roberto Osimani and Mico Martic

**Objectives:**
Pass: with one game time. Pass: with two game times.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
From 5 to 15 minutes.

**Number of players:**
At least 6 players: they arranged themselves in a circle.

**Materials:**
1 ball.

**Progression:**
Following the example of the diagram: the player (3) passes to a teammate (3) and goes to pressure him. The other player (6) receives the ball and performs a one-two with a nearby teammate. Player 6, having received the ball, starts the movement again. TX01.1 ? Inside of the foot. TX01.2 ? Sole reception + inside pass. TX01.3 ? Sole reception + outside pass. POINTS OF ATTENTION: Ensure the execution of the pass is precise.
