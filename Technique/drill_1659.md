# Drill 1659

**Short description:**
Collective Tactics

**Objectives:**
1-on-1 situation. Situations of: dribbling + passing; dribbling + shooting.

**Area (mxm):**
20x28

**Duration (min):**
From 8' to 20'

**Number of players:**
8 players: 2 goalkeepers, 2 teams made up of 3 players.

**Materials:**
1 ball. Bibs to define the teams. Cones: to mark the 20x28 meter field (move the goals to the edge of the penalty area).

**Progression:**
A match is played with futsal rules. The special rule is that each possession cannot include more than 3 passes. Within 3 passes, a shot on goal must be attempted. The goalkeeper's goal kick is not counted as a pass.

**Variations:**
Vary the number of passes available for the team.

**Categories:**
Technique - 1v1
