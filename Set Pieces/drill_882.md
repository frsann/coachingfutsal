**Progression:**
BASE MOVEMENT: T1: A plays the ball down the line to D, and moves towards the ball. T2: D passes the ball to A, who lets it run for pivot B, who has moved away down the line. After letting the ball run, player A moves to the center of the field. SOLUTIONS: S1: Pivot B passes the ball to A, who shoots at goal. S2: B passes the ball to D, who shoots. POINTS OF ATTENTION: Player A must move towards D to attract the attention of defender L. The pass from D to A must be on the outside foot, precise and strong. TACTICAL SITUATION: Near the opponent's goal, within 15 meters.
