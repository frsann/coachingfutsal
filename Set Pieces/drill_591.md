# Drill 591

**Short description:**
Free kick 11

**Objectives:**
Practicing a free kick

**Area (mxm):**
20x20 (field size)

**Duration (min):**
5-10 minutes

**Number of players:**
4 attackers vs 4 defenders + goalkeeper

**Materials:**
Balls, printed T-shirts

**Progression:**
Forward number 4 passes the ball to forward number 5. Forward number 5 passes the ball back to forward number 4. At a certain point, forward number 3 positions himself to see an open path to the goal. Forward number 4 passes the ball to forward number 3 who shoots at the goal immediately.

**Variations:**


**Categories:**
Set Pieces - Attack, Set Pieces - Defense
