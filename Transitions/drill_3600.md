# Drill 3600

**Short description:**
Real 5v5 game.

**Objectives:**
Improve the game in transitions.

**Area (mxm):**
40x20

**Duration (min):**
12

**Number of players:**
At least 10 players

**Materials:**
Balls, markers

**Progression:**
A real 5v5 game. When a player from the attacking team leaves the boundaries of the field wherever they wish, another player enters the game wherever they wish. It is sufficient for the player leaving the game to raise their hand at the moment they cross the line of the field.

**Variations:**
Adjust the size of the field as needed. Change the number of touches as needed. And the defense can make substitutions.

**Categories:**
Transitions - Attack, Transitions - Defense, Warm up - Tactics
