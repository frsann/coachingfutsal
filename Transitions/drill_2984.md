# Drill 2984

**Short description:**
Transitions 2x1+3x2+4x3

**Objectives:**
Transitions

**Area (mxm):**
40x20

**Duration (min):**
12

**Number of players:**
All the players (7+2)

**Materials:**
Balloons, kites

**Progression:**
It starts with a free throw. 2x1, 3x2, 4x3. Immediately play back to the goalkeeper to speed up transitions.

**Variations:**
Finalize with a touch.

**Categories:**
Transitions - Attack, Transitions - Defense
