# Drill 81

**Short description:**
Combined task for defense work and numerical superiority

**Objectives:**
Defense - Superiority 2 x 1

**Area (mxm):**
40 x 20 (3 x 3 in 25x20 - 2 x 1 in 15x20)

**Duration (min):**
2 series of 6 minutes (rotation of players in both series)

**Number of players:**
9 Players + 2 Goalkeepers

**Materials:**
Goals, balls, vests

**Progression:**
In one area of the field, defense is practiced (half field â€“ high zone). In another area, there is a 2 vs. 1 situation. There are no throw-ins or corner kicks. The goalkeeper of the team in possession always puts the ball into play. When a goal is scored, the goalkeeper of the team that scored starts the play.

**Variations:**
Limit time in the 2 vs 1 situation. Limit touches and time in the 3 vs 3 situation.

**Categories:**
Positional Defense, Transitions - Attack, Transitions - Defense
