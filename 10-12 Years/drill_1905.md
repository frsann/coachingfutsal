# Drill 1905

**Short description:**
Individual technique

**Objectives:**
Shot: 2-player game. With the pivot in the central position: backward pass.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
From 8' to 20'

**Number of players:**
At least 7 players: 5 outfield players, 2 goalkeepers.

**Materials:**
Various balls.

**Progression:**
The game is played in half-court, with players with the ball positioned at the corners and their teammate in the center. A player passes the ball to the teammate in the center, who returns it to make them shoot at the goal. Then they switch positions. The player in the center receives from another teammate and sets them up to shoot at the goal, then they switch roles, and so on. POINTS OF ATTENTION: The return pass from the pivot must be made with the sole of the foot and backwards.

**Categories:**
10-12 years, Technique - Shoting
