# Drill 3124

**Short description:**
Mini tournament

**Objectives:**
Development of a competitive spirit.

**Area (mxm):**
40x20

**Duration (min):**
20

**Number of players:**
14

**Materials:**
Balls and markers

**Progression:**
Let's divide the players into multiple teams. Matches 3:3, 4:4.

**Variations:**
An additional goal at the side position in the center.

**Categories:**
10-12 years, 6-8 years, 8-10 years
