# Drill 952

**Short description:**
Exercise 2

**Objectives:**
Advanced closure.

**Area (mxm):**
Goal area.

**Duration (min):**
3

**Number of players:**
1 goalkeeper

**Materials:**
Balls, 6 pins

**Progression:**
The goalkeeper consecutively blocks the coach's shots. With each save, P advances by one goal until he is about 2 meters from the coach. The cones are placed 2 meters apart.

**Variations:**
None

**Categories:**
Positional Defense
