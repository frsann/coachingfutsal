# Drill 812

**Short description:**
Combination Exercise 2

**Objectives:**
Shot Saving High and Low / Recovery to standing position / Blocking technique, shape and timing / Saving and or blocking at far post / Foot movement

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
10-15 Minutes

**Number of players:**
4 Players and 1 Goalkeeper

**Materials:**
Ball, Tennis Ball, Cones, Bench or Table

**Progression:**
Goalkeeper will dive across goal to save shot from player 2.Player 3 will pass to Player 4; goalkeeper will recover to standing position to narrow angle and block player 4 shot.Player 2 will shoot high at opposite post, goalkeeper will move across goal and save shot.Player 5 will then hit tennis ball at bench/table, goalkeeper will move across goal and using block shape make save.

**Variations:**
Alternate excercise to otherside of court.Vary combination of shots.

**Categories:**
Positional Defense
