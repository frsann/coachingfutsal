# Drill 2929

**Short description:**
4v4 defending with 2 wing zones for attackers. Attackers in wing zones can't move out of the zones. Only way to move out of the zone is when in possession the winger needs to put hand up and neutralize his direct opponent (that defender has to find another attacker to defend) and then the game is 4v4 without any restrictions and lasts until goal is scored or until defenders win the ball.

**Objectives:**
Individual defending and defensive rotation when player is beaten on the wing. 
Weak and strong side. 
Defensive lines.

**Area (mxm):**
28x20

**Duration (min):**
8 minutos

**Number of players:**
8 + 2 GKs

**Materials:**
Balls, bibs, flat markers

**Progression:**
When defenders win the ball they have 6 seconds to finish the action.

**Variations:**
When wing player puts hand up his direct opponent can't join the game until his team wins possession.

**Categories:**
Positional Defense
