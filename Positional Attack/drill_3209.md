# Drill 3209

**Short description:**
Individual and collective tactics, simple 3vs3 with the objective of bringing the ball to the baseline.

**Objectives:**
Attack: all technical gestures except for the shot on goal. Defense: marking related to the ball and opponent.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
Repetitions of 3'-4'. You can perform 2 to 4 repetitions. Recovery depends on athletic goals.

**Number of players:**
6 players: 3 defenders and 3 forwards

**Materials:**
1 ball. Bibs: to define the teams. Cones to define the line where the goal is scored.

**Progression:**
The purpose of the exercise is for the team in possession of the ball to score a goal by carrying the ball beyond the goal line. The rules are the normal game rules. The winner of the challenge is whoever scores the most goals.

**Variations:**
Technical limitations can be introduced for the attack, e.g. limitations on touches, etc.

**Categories:**
Positional Attack, Technique - 1v1, Warm up - Technique
