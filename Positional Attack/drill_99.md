# Drill 99

**Short description:**
Pressing, 4vs4 on a 20x20, the goal is to knock down the cones with the ball.

**Objectives:**
Pressing.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
4x3

**Number of players:**
4v4

**Materials:**
Balls, marked T-shirts, cones

**Progression:**
The game is played 4vs4 on half of the field. Each team defends 4 cones located on the goal line of their half. A goal counts only when a cone is hit with the ball. Full-field pressing is mandatory. At the start of each action, opposing players must respect a 5-meter distance. The team that scores more "goals" wins.

**Variations:**
Reduce the number of touches. Limit the area from which a goal can be scored.

**Categories:**
Positional Attack - Exit Pressing, Positional Defense - Pressing
