# Drill 2221

**Short description:**
3v3 on 28x20

**Objectives:**
Defense of Exchanges. Exit pressing. First line imporvement.

**Area (mxm):**
28x20

**Duration (min):**
10-15 min

**Number of players:**
All the players

**Materials:**
Bibs, balls, cones

**Progression:**
3v3, 28x20, Defense with first line at 30m- man to man (individual defense)- pure zone marking (doubling)- on your own (Defense of Exchanges)- quick ball recover (intensity, jumps)- ball recover with patience, organisation and intensity

**Variations:**
- Game always starts from the Gk- One touch goal only- "Up" goal only

**Categories:**
Positional Attack, Positional Attack - Exit Pressing, Positional Defense, Positional Defense - Pressing
