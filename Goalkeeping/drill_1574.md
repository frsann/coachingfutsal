# Drill 1574

**Short description:**
Goalkeeper - Save

**Objectives:**
Double parade with forward movement.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
From 5' to 10'

**Number of players:**
1 goalkeeper, 1 forward.

**Materials:**
1 ball. 2 cones positioned at the height of the posts, about 2.5 meters from the baseline.

**Progression:**
The forward shoots, the goalkeeper defends behind the line of cones, then goes to save the shot above the line of cones. Shoot at the goal while moving forward. Check when he finds the balance to defend the shot.

**Variations:**
The goalkeeper saves the first shot behind the line of cones, then goes around the outer cone and saves the shot over the line of cones.

**Categories:**
Goalkeeping
