# Drill 797

**Short description:**
Goalkeeper - Position

**Objectives:**
Movements, sense of position, save.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
From 5’ to 10’

**Number of players:**
1 goalkeeper, 4 forwards.

**Materials:**
At least 4 balls. Cones: a goal is set up in the center of the field.

**Progression:**
The goalkeeper must defend the goal from both sides. The forwards are arranged in a diamond shape around the goal. The players aligned with the goal pass the ball, while the others facing the goal shoot at the net. After saving the shot from one side, the goalkeeper immediately moves to the other goal to save the next shot. Perform the exercise with rhythm, and the goalkeeper must make at least 4 consecutive saves. Minimize the time between one shot and another.
