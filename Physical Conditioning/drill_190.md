# Drill 190

**Short description:**
Athletic Training – Speed

**Objectives:**
Development of speed and quickness

**Area (mxm):**
40x20

**Duration (min):**
4 repetitions of all circuits

**Number of players:**
At least 1 player. The players are divided into 4 groups.

**Materials:**
Low hurdles or sticks and cones to form the paths.

**Progression:**
The exercise is divided into 4 stations, with the player groups working simultaneously. Station 1: perform rapid skipping over 4 low obstacles; then high skipping for 3 meters; upon reaching the next 4 low obstacles, again perform rapid skipping; finish with a 6-meter sprint. Station 2: perform leaps forward for 8 meters, then do a 10-meter sprint. Station 3: zig-zag running with lateral movements, following the path defined by cones; at the end of the path, perform a 10-meter sprint. Station 4: perform defensive sliding between the 5 obstacles, followed by a 20-meter sprint. At the end, the groups switch stations.
