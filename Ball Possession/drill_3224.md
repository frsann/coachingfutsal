# Drill 3224

**Short description:**
Possession with 3 colors. Ball in hand.

**Objectives:**
Ball possession.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
12

**Number of players:**
9

**Materials:**
Balls, small houses, cones.

**Progression:**
The game is played with a limit of 2 ball touches. Possession rules with 3 colors. Upon a change of possession, the game is played with the ball that was in a defender's hand. To intercept the ball, the defender must have the ball they were playing with in hand.

**Variations:**
After 20 consecutive passes, one remains to defend for an additional turn. The second round is played with 1 touch, with 15 passes, double round.

**Categories:**
Ball Possession
