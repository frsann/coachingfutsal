# Drill 122

**Short description:**
Collective tactics

**Objectives:**
Concept of wide play through switching the play. Quality of passing and closing passing lanes

**Area (mxm):**
14x28, with the central area having a width of 6-8 meters.

**Duration (min):**
From 10' to 20'

**Number of players:**
12 players: 3 teams of 4 players

**Materials:**
1 ball. Cones: to define the field. Bibs to define the 3 teams.

**Progression:**
Two teams position themselves in the outer areas of the field. In one of these areas, there is a 4 versus 2 game, while at the other end there are another 4 players, with 2 defenders in the center. The team in possession of the ball can pass the ball to the other group of 4 players after making 4 passes, without it being intercepted by the players in the center. If the two in the center intercept, there is a change of roles, and the team that lost the ball goes to defend. If the ball reaches the other group, the two in the center switch to the 4 versus 2, and the other 2 position themselves in the center. It's played with 2 touches.

**Variations:**
Vary the playing space or the number of ball touches.

**Categories:**
Ball Possession, Group Actions - Build up
