# Drill 3496

**Short description:**
Proper Futsal Warm-up

**Objectives:**
Decision-making, passing, 1-2 touch play

**Area (mxm):**
12x12

**Duration (min):**
10

**Number of players:**
8

**Materials:**
put balls near the square

**Progression:**
- 6 green players with the objective to keep possession of the ball;- green players don't have to stay in the corner but they can randomly move on the sides of the square- 1 green player can stai into the square, they can exchange the position- Green players have only 2 touches on the ball;2 red players with the objective to get a hold of the ball or intercept it;Â· If the ball is intercepted, players switch roles (the one, who lost the ball becomes a defender and the one, who won the ball become an attacking player)
