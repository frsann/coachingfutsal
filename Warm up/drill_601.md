# Drill 601

**Short description:**
Warming

**Objectives:**
Preparation for subsequent work.

**Area (mxm):**
Room

**Duration (min):**
15'

**Number of players:**
12

**Materials:**
Balls

**Progression:**
Movements without and with the ball. Passes in pairs, all over the court. Possession: 6x6 to make 10 passes in one's own field. Stretching.

Warm up - Tactics, Warm up - Technique
