# Drill 2240

**Short description:**
Game 8x8

**Objectives:**
- When the ball is carried over the line, the team is awarded one goal
- playing with 3 touches

**Area (mxm):**
Across the field

**Duration (min):**
2 halves x 7 minutes

**Number of players:**
12-15 players

**Materials:**
Two balls

**Progression:**


**Variations:**
- Add a second ball - play in 2 touches

**Categories:**
Warm up - General activation
