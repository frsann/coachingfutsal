# Drill 2687

**Short description:**
Each player with their own ball.

**Objectives:**
Know the field and the ball.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
3-4'

**Number of players:**
10 + 2 goalkeepers

**Materials:**
Balloons

**Progression:**
Each player has their own ball. They move freely with the ball to become familiar with the field and the ball.

**Categories:**
Warm up - Technique
