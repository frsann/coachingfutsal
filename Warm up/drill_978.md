Easy to perform, it is one of the oldest tests to evaluate high jump ability. From a standing position with the side next to the wall, mark with the outstretched arm the highest point on the wall that can be touched with the fingers. Then, from a position with legs bent at a 90Â° angle, perform a jump and mark the height reached on the wall with your fingers. Perform 3 trials, with 3-minute intervals, then choose the best one.
