# Drill 2994

**Short description:**
Warm-up after the mobility physical trainer's work.

**Objectives:**
Prepare for the central part of the training. Improve peripheral vision and play 3. Also improve the goalkeepers' timing and everyone's assist.

**Area (mxm):**
40x20

**Duration (min):**
Two sets of 10' with 3' recovery.

**Number of players:**
11 players

**Materials:**
10 balls, vests.

**Progression:**
First part: 10' time with free touches. The goalkeeper passes the ball to one of the attacking players. To pass the ball to the other side, it must touch all 3 players plus the goalkeeper. The ball can be passed to the goalkeeper as many times as desired. Defenders cannot enter the area. Second part: 10' time Limited touches 2. Now, the team that defends and recovers the ball can score goals and has free touches. Goals can be scored and it is allowed to enter the area.

**Variations:**
* Position 2 jokers outside on the halves of the court who enter when the defending team recovers the ball. Now it's 2 touches.* The joker plays only in the middle of the court as support to get out of possible high pressure.

**Categories:**
Warm up - Technique
