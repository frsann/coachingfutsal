# Drill 635

**Short description:**
Individual tactics

**Objectives:**
Improvement of the passing-receiving chain; timing of disengagement based on ball circulation.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
From 5' to 10'

**Number of players:**
6 players: 4 passers, 1 defender, and 1 attacker.

**Materials:**
1 ball. Cones: to define the square (8x8 meters). Bibs: to define, in the center, the attacker and the defender.

**Progression:**
Inside the rectangle, there is a defender and an attacker playing. The players on the outside each play on one side with a requirement of two touches. The goal is to pass the ball to the attacker in the center of the rectangle. The defender in the center must prevent the attacker from receiving the ball.

**Variations:**
The players outside are playing with one touch.

**Categories:**
Warm up - Technique
