# Drill 2844

**Short description:**
The Futsal Way

**Objectives:**


**Area (mxm):**
40x20

**Duration (min):**


**Number of players:**
12 + 2P

**Materials:**


**Progression:**
The group is divided into 2 teams of 6, plus the 2 goalkeepers. 1- Initially, a 2vs2 + 2 joker design is played available to the attackers, positioned at the sides of the goal, which can be utilized without any limitation. 2- At the end of the play, the goalkeeper must make a kick to one of the two attackers positioned at the edge of the opposite penalty area. 3- After a non-contestable control, the two attackers must overcome the pressure from the two defenders, with the goal of serving one of their two teammates in the opposite half of the field. 4- The one who made the vertical pass will participate in the 3vs2 with the four players who had played step 1. 5- At the end of the play, the two defenders will attack the opposite goal against the remaining opponent in the other half of the field for a 2vs1, which will conclude the series. At the end of the series, the rotation of pairs is such that those who attacked will become jokers, those who were defending will switch to attacking, while the two jokers will go to defend. Each pair must assume all roles (offensive and defensive) four times, for a total of twenty-four series.

**Variations:**


**Categories:**
Group Actions - Build up, Group Actions - Finishing, Positional Attack, Positional Attack - Exit Pressing, Positional Defense, Positional Defense - Pressing, Transitions - Attack, Transitions - Defense
