# Drill 1646

**Short description:**
Each player has a different color. Defense cannot be played against an opponent of the same color.

**Objectives:**
Perception, defensive organization, positioning correction.

**Area (mxm):**
40x20

**Duration (min):**
10-12

**Number of players:**
All players

**Materials:**
Balls, marked T-shirts

**Progression:**
4v4 with goalkeepers on 40x20, each team is made up of players with different colored jerseys. They cannot defend against an opponent of their own color.

**Variations:**
Only a first-touch goal counts.

**Categories:**
Group Actions - Build up, Positional Attack, Positional Defense
