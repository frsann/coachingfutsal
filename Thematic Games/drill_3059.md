# Drill 3059

**Short description:**
Themed games. Diamond Joker game.

**Objectives:**
Theme games.

**Area (mxm):**
40x20

**Duration (min):**
12

**Number of players:**
All players (10+2)

**Materials:**
Balls, houses, cones.

**Progression:**
A 3 against 3 game is played, players can play with wildcards. Wildcards cannot score goals and only play one-touch within their own square.

**Variations:**
Finalize with a touch.

**Categories:**
Thematic Games
