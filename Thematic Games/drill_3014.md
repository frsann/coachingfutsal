# Drill 3014

**Short description:**
Theme game - juggling 2 balls.

**Objectives:**
Individual TE-TA, perception.

**Area (mxm):**
40x20

**Duration (min):**
Twelve

**Number of players:**
All players (8+2)

**Materials:**
Balloons, houses

**Progression:**
Players with the ball in hand cannot attack or defend.

**Variations:**
Finalize in one touch.

**Categories:**
Thematic Games
