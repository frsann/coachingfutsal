# Drill 3025

**Short description:**
Theme games. 4x4 + 1 midfield.

**Objectives:**
Themed games.

**Area (mxm):**
20x20

**Duration (min):**
12

**Number of players:**
All players (7/9+1)

**Materials:**
Balloons, houses.

**Progression:**
The joker plays with 1 touch and only on the half-field line. Every time possession changes, to attack the goal, it must be played with the joker.

**Variations:**
Finalize with one touch.

**Categories:**
Thematic Games
